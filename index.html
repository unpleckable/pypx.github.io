How to use AI responsibily

AI or artificial intelligence is a very powerful tool which should be used responsibly. AI should assist with tasks, not complete them for you. Because artificial intelligence is not perfect, when asking complex questions there is a high chance that it will get your prompt incorrect or provide false information. This is commonly known as hallucination. You are accountable for what you ask the model, all data you input is sent off to a server and saved, so avoid inputting personal information to an AI chatbot as if the service was hacked your data would be leaked.

Responsible use of AI in schools

AI should be used to ideate and assist you with minor tasks, but it should not be used to write you an entire essay or complete an assignment for you.
